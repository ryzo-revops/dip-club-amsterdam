Benefits

Why it works

Heat exposure

Saunas boost cardiovascular health, flush toxins, and trigger deep relaxation through heat stress adaptation.

Cold exposure

Ice baths reduce inflammation, sharpen focus, and build mental resilience. Your nervous system will thank you.

Breathwork

Controlled breathing calms anxiety, increases energy, and prepares your body to handle the cold with ease.

Time in nature

Being outdoors lowers cortisol, boosts immunity, and restores attention. Screens off, senses on.

Shared experiences

Vulnerability builds trust faster than small talk ever will. Suffer together, bond for life.

How to Get Started

Three steps to your first dip

No application. No membership fee. Just show up and be willing to feel something.

1

Join the group

Hop into our WhatsApp community. That's where we share event details, locations, and the occasional cold-water meme.

2

Show up Saturday

We meet every Saturday morning. Bring a towel, warm clothes for after, and the willingness to feel alive. We provide the ice.

3

Take the plunge

Guided breathing. Cold water. Warm people. You'll feel like you're dying for 30 seconds, then more alive than ever. Repeat weekly.
